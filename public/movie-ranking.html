Belen's Movie Ranking

My top 3 movies of all time!

Girl, Interrupted

A very moody motion picture starring two of my favorite actressess; Angelina Jolie & Brittany Murphy.
It shines a light on women trying to navigate through certain mental health issues. 8/10

Rio

This movie brings me back to fond memories with my younger siblings.
We would rewatch it over and over but would enjoy it as if it was our first time experiencing it. 9/10

VHS

This is my all time favorite suspense film. It's a compilation of a bunch of eerie, unexplained VHS
casettes that someone randomly finds. 7/10
